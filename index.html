About Us

Paradigm Initiative is a non-profit social enterprise that builds an ICT-enabled support system and advocates digital rights in order to improve livelihoods for under-served youth. We believe in the power of technology to create positive change and bridge the digital divide.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs aimed at equipping individuals and communities with the skills and knowledge needed in the digital age. Our programs include:

  • Digital Inclusion Workshops
  • Advocacy for Digital Rights
  • Technology Training Initiatives

Scholarships

Paradigm Initiative offers scholarships to empower individuals in pursuing education and building a career in the digital field. Our scholarships aim to support talented and motivated individuals who aspire to make a difference through technology.
